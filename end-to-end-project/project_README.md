# Financial Analysis and Forecasting Portfolio Project

## Project Overview

This project is an end to end financial analysis and forecasting case study built with Python. It uses simulated company financial data to analyze revenue performance, profitability, budget variance, customer concentration, working capital, cash flow, and monthly revenue forecasting.

The goal is to show how Python can be used to turn raw financial data into business insights, forecasts, and executive recommendations.

---

## Business Questions

This project answers the following questions:

* What drives revenue growth?
* Which products, regions, and channels generate the most revenue?
* Which products have high sales but weaker margins?
* Are discounts helping revenue or hurting profitability?
* Where is the company beating or missing budget?
* Which customers contribute the most revenue?
* How efficiently does the company convert sales into cash?
* Which forecasting model best predicts monthly revenue?
* What actions should management take?


## Key Findings

* The company generated strong FY2025 performance, with approximately **$119.1M in revenue**, **$61.4M in gross profit**, and a **52% gross margin**.

* **Subscription** and **Data Platform** are the strongest product lines because they combine high revenue with high margins.

* **Hardware** generates meaningful revenue but has the weakest gross margin, making it a priority for pricing, cost, or supplier review.

* **North America** is the largest revenue market, while **Latin America** has the longest collection period, suggesting a working capital issue.

* Higher discount levels are associated with weaker margins and higher return rates, suggesting that broad discounting may be hurting profitability.

* All product lines finished below budget, with the largest shortfalls coming from **Subscription** and **Data Platform**.

* Customer concentration risk is low because the top customers account for a small share of total revenue.

* **Enterprise customers** generate the highest revenue per customer, making them the most valuable customer segment.

* **Prophet** produced the best forecasting performance based on out-of-sample MAPE and was used for the final 12-month revenue forecast.

* Recommended actions include expanding high-margin product lines, reviewing Hardware profitability, tightening discount strategy, improving collections in Latin America, and using Prophet for future revenue planning.
